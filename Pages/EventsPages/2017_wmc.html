WORLD MUSIC CONCERT

Townsville Civic Theatre 5 September, 8pm

“Music is the social act of communication between people, a gesture of friendship, the strongest there is.”

– MALCOLM ARNOLD

A unique performance conceptualised and developed here in Townsville by the Barrier Reef Orchestra highlighting the great diversity of cultural music from around the globe, featuring several international artists. So pack away the passport take the trip of a life time and never leave home.

Adults $38 | Concession $34 | NQE Members $32 | Students $15
