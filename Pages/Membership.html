Become a Member

North Queensland Ensembles Inc. is the administration body of the Barrier Reef Orchestra, is excited to invite you to become a member and enjoy fantastic member benefits.

Memberships costs just $30 per annum for an adult.

Additional membership options are available depending on your circumstances, these are annuals fees of $60 for a family*, $15 for Full-time Students, under 18 and concession holders.*

Becoming a member of the Orchestra entitles you to members only updates about the Orchestra and concessions on event tickets.

In addition to community members, the Orchestra is always on the hunt for new and talented artists who would like to perform with the Orchestra and upcoming events. The same membership form can be used to register your interest to perform in the Orchestra.
