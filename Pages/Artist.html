Artists


Summary of the calibre of artists that perform in the orchestra, often are from outside of the Townsville region, arrangement with Australian Concerto and Vocal Competition for soloists to perform etc.


Yoshika is a cello player that has performed concertos and recitals throughout Australia, Austria, France, Italy, Japan, the UK and USA.

Conductor Mario Dobernig grew up in a small village in the Austrian Alps before establishing his professional career as a percussionist in Graz (Austria)

Richard McIntyre sustains a long career as bassoonist, music educator, conductor and arranger. McIntyre has worked at ANU for over a quarter of a century

Townsville harpist Leah Xiang Li enjoys a diverse career as an orchestral and chamber musician, soloist, instrumental and primary school language teacher.

Jennifer Bradstreet completed a Bachelor of Music with Honours at the Sydney Conservatorium, before taking up a scholarship at St Andrews/Oxford in the UK.

Raymond Yong, born in Malaysia, emigrated to Australia at the age of two. He had musical training in Perth, performing regularly in piano recitals from an early age.

David Hudson is an Australian Aboriginal musician, entertainer and artist. Hudson is a multi-instrumentalist, he was taught to play the didgeridoo from an early age.

Domenico is one of BROs most experienced /versatile multicultural entertainers. He has performed in almost every possible style since he started his career in 1971.
