About Us

Established in 2000, the Barrier Reef Orchestra is Townsville's own community orchestra. The Orchestra was formed through a local group of passionate individuals wanting JCU's music Students to have an Orchestra to play in. 17 years later the Orchestra has grown and evolved to include members of the T150 spectacular army band, passionate local artists and professionals from outside of the Townsville region.

The Orchestra has formed valuable relationships with members of the music industry to support an agreement with the Australian Concherto and Vocal Competition. This competition offers a unique opportunity for solo vocalists and instrumentalists to make a name for themself and opens up opportunities to perform within an Orchestra. Each year the Barrier Reef Orchestra provides opportunities for winners of the competition to perform within the Orchestra.
