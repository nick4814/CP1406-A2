Become a Benefactor

With a philanthropic gift of $200 or more (we leave it up to you) you become an Orchestra Benefactor and be placed on our Benefactor roll for twelve months. This roll will be published in each concert program and on our website for twelve months. As a benefacor you will also receive two complimentary tickest to our next event, be placed on the mailing list for our newsletter and will be a guest at our annual Season's Launch function.

All donations are 100% tax deductable. For more information contact Simon McConnell on 0418 776 961 or via email at brotownsville@gmail.com
