Jennifer Bradstreet

Jennifer Bradstreet completed a Bachelor of Music with Honours at the Sydney Conservatorium, before taking up a summer scholarship at St Andrews and Oxford in the UK, and then moving to London in 2010. She became a student at the Royal College of Music under British flautist Susan Milan and has since graduated.

In 2013, Jennifer accepted a Masters scholarship under James Kortum and Aldo Baerten (Belgium) at the Sydney Conservatorium, where she will complete her studies this year.
